All about the outdoors

My name is Dazarian Williams and I am a self described explorer. I often find myself traveling the natural world by foot, by Boat, by car, by bike, any way I can get around. I plan on creating a story worth reading so follow along to stay updated on the latest information, and to follow my travels around the globe. We like to have fun here, so be kind to others and join the the fellowship, we are in this together!

Journal Entry 1:

Just finished rowing to a mysterious island, the crew on the ship I was with refused to move any closer, and said something about a local supersition. Allegedly anyone who steps foot on the island comes face to face with a strange creature that follows you everywhere. They say coming into contact with it brings you misfortune and some have even gone missing on the island.








Journal Entry 2:

I set up base camp just off the beach of the island, there wasnt much to work with nearby but I was able to setup a samll fifteen foot cottage, to work out of. It took all day and I had no signs of life minus the harbor seals and occassional birds. I believe the local superstition might be some sort of hoax, or a legend that has died with the times.








Journal Entry 3:

I just solved the mystery, the creature that was mytholigized was a dog, it's cute.
